Meet The Crew!







Cattson is the leader of the band of Meowzers. He is insanely talented and brings a lot of joy with him everywhere he goes! He is the lead singer and guitarist, so be sure to come early to get front row seats so you don't miss out on his actions. Look out though as sometimes he may get quite wild!







Jermery is a charming young cat who is only 2 years old, young for his age for being a drummer! His cuteness will overwhelm your little hearts as he drums his melodic tunes to sound of your heartbeat. When you meet him, have an ambulance nearby for reports of getting a heartattacks are common simply because of his stunning cuteness!







Queen is the lead vocalist for Meowzers. Although she might look adorable on the outside, inside she packs a mean punch! She is usually known for being quite bossy and demanding, but the fans love it! If you ever manage to get her to open up, She can actually be quite nice to you, well if you able to survive her in the first place!






Freddie is the pianist and he can play the most insane chords! He might be a little shy at first but once you get to know him he can be a real party animal. His pianist skills usually are the star of the show, so make sure you don't miss out! Fun fact: he always carry a flask of whiskey wherever he goes, along with his famous saying "No matter the occasion, You can always use a Drink!"







Lastly, Aloe is the violinist for Meowzers. When she strum her violin, the sound it emits will dazzle and make you tear up. The melody she produces is so beautiful that she was awarded dozens of awards of international recognition just from her skills. Aloe is also one of the kindest cat you'll ever meet. You'll never feel harmed whenever you are with her.
